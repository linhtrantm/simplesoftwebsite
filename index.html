  • GPS-verified delivery routes & shipper tracking

A multi-tenant SaaS platform designed for Vietnamese repair shops of all sizes. SimpleRMS streamlines the entire repair lifecycle — from intake to delivery — with built-in customer communication via Zalo OA and flexible workflows that adapt to any repair industry.
